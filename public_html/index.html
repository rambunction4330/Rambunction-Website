So Why Join
Rambunction?

Team 4330 isn't only about designing, building, and competing with a robot. It’s also about learning about new STEAM fields like computer science, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and visual design. We also practice real-world skills every day of season, such as problem solving, presentation, and teamwork.

Our Current Competiton

A Summary of What's Happening This Season

2017 Competition: FIRST Steamworks

This year’s game, named STEAMWORKS, transports us back in time to the Victorian era with a steampunk theme! Each team has an Airship with two Pilot human players aboard to operate it. Robots can deliver Gears to get Rotors on the Airship working or shoot Fuel units into the Boiler to create steam that powers this Airship. At the end of the game, robots may climb onboard via a Rope to gain extra points and prepare for takeoff. Full steam ahead!

You can check out the season's game manual and more on the FIRST website.
